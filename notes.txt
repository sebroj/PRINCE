Successfully built the Electron C++ addon with the following
series of commands on Windows (starting with just the src and binding.gyp)

node-gyp configure --target=1.6.2 --arch=x64 --dist-url=https://atom.io/download/electron
node-gyp build --target=1.6.2 --arch=x64 --dist-url=https://atom.io/download/electron
